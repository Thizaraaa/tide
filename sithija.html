Shop

Created a comprehensive shop page for "Tide", showcasing a variety of products related to marine conservation and awareness. The shop features a grid layout displaying product images, names, prices, and brief descriptions. Implemented filtering options to sort products by category, price range, and popularity. Each product card includes a "Add to Cart" button, linking directly to the checkout process. The design ensures a smooth browsing experience, encouraging visitors to support the cause through purchases. (Link to shop page)

Checkout

Designed and implemented a secure and user-friendly checkout process for the "Tide" shop. The checkout page includes a summary of the items in the cart, options for shipping methods, and a form for billing and shipping information. Emphasis was placed on creating a streamlined, step-by-step checkout experience to minimize cart abandonment and ensure a smooth purchasing process for customers. (Link to checkout page)
