Tasks Completed

Homepage

Developed the homepage for the "Tide" website, dedicated to exploring and raising awareness about life below water. The homepage serves as an engaging introduction to the site's various sections, offering a comprehensive summary of each page to guide visitors seamlessly through the content.

The design features a user-friendly navigation bar that ensures easy access to different parts of the website, enhancing the overall user experience. Additionally, a well-crafted footer provides essential information and quick links, further enriching site navigation.

I focused on creating a visually appealing and informative template that aligns with the theme of marine life, using appropriate color schemes and imagery to captivate the audience's attention. This project reflects my commitment to promoting environmental awareness through effective web design and development. (Link to the homepage)

Feedback

In addition to the homepage, I also developed the feedback page for the "Tide" website. This page is designed to facilitate user interaction and gather valuable insights from visitors regarding their experience on the site and their thoughts on the content about life below water.

The feedback page features a clean and intuitive layout, with clearly labeled fields for users to input their comments, suggestions, and ratings. The form includes sections for contact information, feedback categories, and an open text area for detailed responses. I implemented responsive design principles to ensure the page is easily accessible and functional across various devices and screen sizes.

By incorporating this feedback mechanism, the "Tide" website can continuously improve and adapt based on user input, fostering a community of engaged and informed visitors. This project highlights my skills in creating interactive web components that enhance user engagement and contribute to the site's overall mission of promoting marine life awareness. (Link to feedback page)

Content page

I also developed a content page for the "Tide" website, designed like a blog to share articles and updates about life below water. The page features a clean, responsive layout with categories, tags, and a search bar for easy navigation. Each post includes images, multimedia, and social sharing options to engage and inform visitors. This page enhances the site's mission by providing valuable, engaging content about marine life.
